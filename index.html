About E-Summit 2021
E-Summit 2021 - The Annual Flagship Extravaganza hosted by Entrepreneurship Cell of Delhi Technological University.
E-Summit by E-Cell DTU is the largest entrepreneurship promoting event of Northern India. The event has served as host to some of the eminent personalities from myriad industries over the many years. It continues to cultivate and nurture numerous ideas every year, and provide an extraordinary platform putting forward the youth to exchange their ideas with the sharpest brains of the corporate world, giving them innumerous insights and lessons to raise their business acumen.

SUMMIT TIMELINE

LDS

Day 1 begins with the leadership and disruption series wherein some of the most celebrated speakers, entrepreneurs and leaders of the world, share their career journey to inspire the attendees. It is a platform to celebrate the glorious achievements and the impact which they have created through their spectrum of work over the years. Top industrialists of our country impart various tips and techniques that the youth may utilize to make it big in their careers.

CCS

After the inspirational session of Leadership and Disruption Series, we have a session on some of the more unconventional forms of entrepreneurship namely youtube entrepreneurship, various YouTubers, and social media influencers share their experiences with the massive crowd assembled at the Open Amphitheatre. We have had the pleasure of hosting various content creators such as Harsh Beniwal Amit Badhana, Slay point, RJ Abhinav - The FAAD magician, Nazarbattu for this event.

CN

To conclude Day 1 of E-Summit on a lighter note, the Comedy Night is held right after the Content Creator Summit. The evening begins with a short performance of a local comedian followed by the main act and concludes with an improv session with some of the top improv artists of the industry. We have had the pleasure of having numerous top comedians of India, such as Biswa Kalyan Rath, Kenny Sebastien, and Abhishek Upmanyu who have performed and exhilarated the audience with their wit and humor.

CC

Who doesn't like to end a competitive challenge by lifting the winning trophy? An astounding line-up of competitions including Business Plan Competition, Case Study and overnight Business Hackathon waiting for you at E-Summit with numerous rewards to be won. These one of a kind bewildering competitions present our budding entrepreneurs with enormous exposure and learning!

BN

The 2 day Extravaganza ends on a musical note where the highly pulsating crowd dances on the tunes of some of the most popular bands amidst the scintillating lights. Attendees from all over the country dance in unison to cool off their fatigue after attending a plethora of highly educational and informative sessions at the most highly acclaimed entrepreneurship summit of Northern India.
